Website Plan

1. Site Name

Site Name: Lakwe Chamber Of Commerce

Reason: This name represents a chamber focused on business owners in Lakwe, making it clear and relevant for the target audience.

Optional domain: lcc.org

2. Site Purpose

The website will serve as a hub for regional business enthusiasts by providing information, business recommendations, and membership details for the Lakwe Chamber Of Commerce.

3. Scenarios

  • What is the best business location to get for restaurant?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's directors?

4. Color Schema

The selected colors are:

  • #333 (Gray): Used for headings.
  • #004080 (Orange): Used for accents such as links or buttons.
  • #f4f4f9 (Light gray): Used as the background color.

5. Typography

  • sans-serif: Used for headings to give them a professional look.
  • Arial: Used for body text to ensure readability.
